COVID-19 worldwide statistics per inhabitants and other resources

Find here a presentation of Covid-19 data per country and per inhabitants.

The data comes from the endpoint described here (https://github.com/Omaroid/Covid-19-API), which is retrieved from the Johns Hopkins University Center for Systems Science and Engineering.

The data presents daily cases but not the latest figures and is not yet a trustable source for all countries because it must be aggregated.
